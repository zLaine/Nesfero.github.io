Role: Programmer, Designer Platform: Windows PC Engine: Unreal Engine 4 Language: C++ Duration: 10 months Team Size: 1

Project Protocol is a side project that I've been working on for the past year to teach myself the ins and outs of the Unreal Engine (4). It is also my "test center" for implementing game design concepts that I've thought of but haven't had the resources to do so. Everything in it is 100% me. To me it is an exercise in level design and programming. I create the necessary assets for each environment and try to have them look as visually appealing to the player as possible. Then I import them and stage them. I head into the blueprint editor and use the node tools to create new materials and program. After gameplay comes rigging and animation. I try to not wear too many hats when doing game development but when you are working by yourself sometimes picking up new skills to make something yourself is more alluring then finding someone else to create for you. One day I'll have some sort of trailer to show for what has been secretly rendering and compiling constantly in my dorm for quite a long while...
